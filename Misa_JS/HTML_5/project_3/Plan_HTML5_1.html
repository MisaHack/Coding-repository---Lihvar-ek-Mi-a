PLAN OF APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT

List of future projects

by Lihvarček Miša

Following is the list of projects that are planed for realising in near future by a small group of entusiastic friends and developers who love technology. Goal is to make applications that are useful to someone or are at least fun to make and use.

D&D Character Sheet

Because all of us, in our team, love video and board games this is obvious choice. Also we particularly like RPG (Role Playing Games) so this is our love letter to the genre and more precisely "Dungeons and Dragons", granddaddy of them all.

Ecology Web Page

By suggestion and idea of our member Miloš Panić, great lover of nature and health of it's own country, we have plan of realising web page with such theme. What will be content of the page and it's look and feel is still in discussion. But one is for sure, education and information of Serbian citizens about problems of surrounding environment is the smallest thing we can do to prevent further polution, educate people and prepare safe ground for future generations.

Atharon Conquest

This is actually a real physical board game. It came straight from the novel "Atharon: Beginning" and feather of the same author, Nikola Kojić. It looks like chess, but fields are more like hexagons. Like chess, it has figures but there are some twists in their behaviour and how they play. Game is relatively complex to master, but easy to pick up and play. So I will be honored that we are going to make it digital, and push it to 21. century.

Game of Life

By suggestion of one of our programmers Marko Jovanović, we will realise this quite interesting "self playing game" that simulates how one population grows and evolves over time by using simple rules. Game itself is the idea of one mathematician.
